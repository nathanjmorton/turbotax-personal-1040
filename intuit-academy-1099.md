# Intuit Turbotax Personal Income Tax 1040

- 4 versions:
1. 1040
2. 1040-SR (senior)
3. 1040-NR (non citizen)
4. 1040-X (amendment)

- determine adjusted gross income (AGI) including income and allowable (above the line) deductions
- AGI line 11
- can reduce AGI with standard or itemized deduction expenses including but not limited to:
  - mortgage interest
  - state and local income tax or sales tax
  - charity
  - medical more than 7.5% of AGI

### Recent Updates to 1040
- standard deduction  
  - single 14.6k
  - married 29.2k
  - head of house 21.9k
- form 8812
  - advanced child tax credit 1.7k
- clean vehicle credit form 8936 and schedule 3
- schedule 3
  - home efficient clean energy and home improvement credits on separate lines
  - prev owned cleaned vehicles from form 8936
  - elective payment amt from form 3800
- self employed health insurance deduction 
  - If you can take the self-employed health insurance deduction on Schedule 1, line 17,  and you can't use the Self-Employed Health Insurance Deduction Worksheet in these  instructions, you will now use Form 7206, instead of Pub. 535, to figure your  deduction. 
- charitable one-time deduction 53k
- rmd age updated from 72 to 73
- public safety officer health insurance premiums 3k from distributions
- exception to 10% tax for early distributions
  - federal disasters
  - terminally ill
  - firefighters meeting age or years of service requirement
- direct file pilot for 2024 tax year
